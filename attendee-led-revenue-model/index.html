How the best events already do it

Paid participation is not new. Packaging it well is.

Meetings as the anchor
The 12-meeting package

Leading hosted-buyer shows sell entry as a package built around a minimum number of guaranteed meetings. The meetings justify the price, the price funds the curation.

Host the few, charge the many
The hosted-buyer model

Super VIP buyers are invited and hosted at no cost. Everyone else pays according to their package. The free seats are what make the paid ones valuable.

Tiered access
Floor to all-access

A minimum pass buys the show floor. Each step up adds meetings, round tables, exhibitor events and an enhanced presence, so the audience self-selects into the revenue.
